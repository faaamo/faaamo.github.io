My drawing on the cloth.
Basic Material : Linen Cloth , Acrylic Paints , Painting Brush ，Markers
Length : 4m Height : 1.4m

The design elements are about the signboards of various
shops in an ancient street in ChangSha city.The street is
a place of historical interest.And the signboards are great
with Chinese characteristics.
The reason why I design this because the particular
signboards gradually disappeared with the injection
of the modern commercial. And I want to use some
artistic patterns to keep the scene.
