The Dong ethnic minority lives primarily in the border regions between Guizhou, Hunan and Hubei Provinces, and it was during the Tang Dynasty (618-907).Their language is a branch of Zhuang- Dong Group of the Sino-Tibetan phylum though many of them also speak Chinese.

The Dong people work on agriculture, forestry and the industrial arts. The women are adept at spinning and embroidering the brocade.The Dong people are also skilled in the arts of painting, engraving and the casting of ornate silver work.
